About Timbertec

Timbertec is a limited liability company that has been existence putting smiles on their customer faces and have recently marked their silver jubilee. Timbertec as the name suggests is a company that transforms wood into finished products in accordance with customer's prefrence.
We have ready made products from various hard wood like the Red oak, Mvule, Vidurian heart pine, White oak among others. We not only deal with wood, we also sell other related products like door locks, ceiling, paints, hinges and furnitures.
We are a one stop shop for interior shopping. Aside from that we also offer fitting services at customer's site for interior decor like ceiling fitting, door fitting, kitchen cabinet, wardrobe e.t.c.



Raw timber at site from various types of trees, both hardwood and softwood. We also have poles used in construction sites.

Our workshops have high quality machine and highly qualified craftsmen, to ensure quality is achieved on our end products.

A Pannel hardwood door being designed by one of our craftsman. We also have school lockers, desks, and cabinets developed upon customer orders.
